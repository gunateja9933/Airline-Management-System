Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change or cancel my booking?

You can change or cancel your booking online through our website or mobile app. Simply log in to your account and navigate to "My Bookings". Cancellation fees may apply depending on your fare type and timing.

What documents do I need for domestic flights?

For domestic flights, you need a government-issued photo ID such as a driver's license, state ID, or passport. Make sure your ID is current and not expired.

What is your baggage policy?

Carry-on bags up to 22x14x9 inches are free. Checked baggage fees vary by destination and fare type. First bag typically costs $30, second bag $40. Weight limit is 50lbs per bag.

How early should I arrive at the airport?

We recommend arriving 2 hours early for domestic flights and 3 hours early for international flights. This allows sufficient time for check-in, security screening, and boarding.

Can I select my seat in advance?

Yes, you can select your seat during booking or later through "Manage Booking". Basic seats are free, while premium seats with extra legroom have an additional fee.
